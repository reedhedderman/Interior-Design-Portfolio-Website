Project 2

The journey through college is one that consists of many decisions, changes, and growth of each individual. The HCOA has guided thousands of students through this journey, and our design intends to enhance the built environment to better support this experience. Creating a building that moves and adapts to the needs of its users seamlessly, is one of our main goals. By considering the effects of each design decision, the site will transform into a space that provides an inspirational learning environment for the future, while still honoring the path of the HCOA.
